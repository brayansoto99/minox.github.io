ABOUT US


Mission

Our mission is to meet the needs of our customers, by casting parts in steels with the highest quality and providing the best service, thus committing ourselves to continuous improvement, maintaining the welfare of our employees.

View

We have the vision to be internationally recognized in the manufacture of steel castings, using the best methods, consolidating a moderate and sustained growth with the effort of all the personnel, in this way we will continue improving the efficiency.

Quality Politics

We are committed to melting pieces in steels meeting the quality standards, the requirements of our customers, and those applicable, through our Quality Management System ISO 9001 in a Process of Continuous Improvement.

Quality Objectives

Comply at least with 90% the delivery of committed pieces per month.
Maintain internal rejection in kilograms under 3% per month.
Keep the external rejection in pieces under 2.50% monthly.
Achieve customer satisfaction at least with a level of 90%
